1.2 Nature of Platform
HeloMed is a technology platform only.
HeloMed does not manufacture, sell, or dispense medicines directly.
Medicines are supplied by independent licensed pharmacies registered on the platform.
HeloMed only facilitates:
* Order placement
* Order routing to pharmacies
* Delivery logistics
* Payment processing
* Healthcare service facilitation

1.3 Services Offered
The platform currently offers:
1. Medicine delivery
2. Healthcare essentials delivery
3. ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) registration assistance
4. Ambulance booking facilitation
5. Healthcare related service enablement
Services may expand or change at any time.

1.4 User Eligibility
Users must:
* Be 18 years or older
* Provide accurate personal details
* Use the platform legally
* Upload valid prescriptions when required
Users are responsible for ensuring prescriptions are issued by registered medical practitioners.

1.5 Prescription Medicines
For prescription drugs:
* A valid prescription must be uploaded
* Pharmacies may verify prescription authenticity
* Orders may be rejected if prescription is invalid
HeloMed reserves the right to cancel suspicious or non-compliant orders.

1.6 Pricing and Charges
Medicine prices are determined by retail pharmacy partners.
HeloMed does not control medicine pricing.
The platform charges only:
* Delivery Fees
* Applicable taxes on delivery fees
Taxes are charged in compliance with Indian tax laws.

1.7 Payment
Users may pay through:
* UPI
* Debit/Credit Cards
* Net Banking
* Wallets
* Payment Gateway providers
Payment processing is handled by third-party payment gateways.
HeloMed does not store full card details.

1.8 Delivery
Orders are delivered by independent delivery partners.
Delivery time depends on:
* Pharmacy readiness

* Delivery partner availability
Delivery timelines shown on the platform are estimates only.
HeloMed is not liable for delays due to:
* Natural disasters

2. SHIPPING & DELIVERY POLICY
HeloMed provides medicine delivery through partner pharmacies and delivery partners.
Delivery Areas
Delivery availability depends on operational areas supported by pharmacies and logistics partners.
Delivery Time
Typical delivery window:
30 minutes to 2 hours depending on location.
Delivery Charges
Delivery charges may vary based on:
* Distance
* Time of delivery
* Demand
* Platform promotions
Taxes are applied only on delivery fees.

3. CANCELLATION POLICY
Customers may cancel orders under the following conditions:
Before Pharmacy Acceptance
Orders may be cancelled without penalty if the pharmacy has not yet accepted the order.

After Pharmacy Acceptance
Once a pharmacy has accepted the order and started preparing the package, cancellation may not be allowed.

After Dispatch
If the order has already been picked up by the delivery partner, cancellation is not permitted.
This is due to the sensitive nature of medicines.

Platform Cancellation
HeloMed may cancel orders if:
* Medicine is unavailable
* Prescription is invalid
* Payment failure occurs
* System errors occur
* Regulatory compliance issues arise
Refunds will be initiated where applicable.

4. RETURN & REFUND POLICY
Due to the safety-sensitive nature of pharmaceutical products, HeloMed follows a strict No-Return Policy.
Medicines cannot be returned once delivered and accepted by the customer.
This policy ensures:
* Patient safety
* Prevention of tampering
* Regulatory compliance

Exceptions
Returns may only be accepted in the following cases:
1. Damaged medicines during delivery
2. Expired medicines received
3. Incorrect medicines delivered
4. Packaging seal broken before delivery
Customers must report such issues within 24 hours of delivery.

Refund Processing
After verification, refunds may be processed via:
* Original payment method
* Wallet credit
* Bank transfer
Refund timeline: 5-7 business days.
